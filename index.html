The heaven for bloggers

iBlog is a website which lets you submit an article which upon approval will be up on our website and you can get a good amount of reach from there. iBlog is a new place on the Internet where people share ideas and stories that are longer than 140 characters and not just for friends. It’s designed for little stories that make your day better and manifestos that change the world. It’s used by everyone from professional journalists to amateur cooks. It’s simple, beautiful, collaborative, and it helps you find the right audience for whatever you have to say.
